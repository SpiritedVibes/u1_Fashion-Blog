On the Street in Brooklyn

Cray was a kaleidoscope of textures and unexpected pairings. From chunky knit sweaters layered over sleek leather trousers to shimmering metallic mini dresses juxtaposed with oversized shearling coats, designers embraced a playful clash of styles. A sense of romanticism infused the collections, with delicate lace details peeking out from beneath chunky knits and flowing silk gowns adorned with intricate embroidery. The overall mood? A confident embrace of individuality, where bold statements meet subtle elegance, leaving us yearning for the season's sartorial delights.

11/11/13

Vintage in Vogue

This season, bold color is making a statement! From vibrant emerald greens to playful fuchsia pinks, designers are embracing a spectrum of hues that are sure to turn heads. Forget the muted tones of winter, it's time to embrace joyful pops of color and let your wardrobe reflect the vibrancy of the new season. Whether you choose a statement dress in a bright hue or simply add a pop of color with a bold accessory, there's a way to incorporate this trend into your personal style.
